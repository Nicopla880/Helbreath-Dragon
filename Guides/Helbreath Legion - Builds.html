Char Builds

*Selecting a character type here only places points in areas as a suggestion.
Determining your character type is up to you and is completly customizable. Levels come easy and stats can be changed once you reach max lvl and have all available points.
  • Helbreath Legion game-play involves Weapons and Spell-Casting.
  • Equip items by double clicking them in your inventory. Release them by double clicking on your Character screen.


  • You may level up in the beginners zone area, the barracks, or in the town until level 50. To continue leveling you must choose to join a town, Elvine or Aresden.
  • To leave a town and go back to the beginner zone you must die. Find a monster in town or attack a city gaurd to be killed.
  • To request citizenship you must talk to the Officer at the City Hall (located in the center of each town).



    • You will have to level up to your wanted stats, this will take using beginner weapons and spells but here is advice in planning a character!

    How much Vitality / Magic / Dexterity should I have?

    Next you must rate the importance of your other stats.
    This will be your own opinion based on your playing style, and favorite balance.
    Open up Character Simulator to plan points.


    Vilality determines health recovery and affects maximum hp most.
    • having 100, 150, or 200 VIT will increase hp up to 100, 150, and 200 respectfully


    Magic determines mana recovery and hitting probability with spells.
    • having 100, 150, or 200 MAG will increase mp up to 100, 150, and 200


    Dexterity is required for using any weapon (50 for 100% skill) other then a staff and determines hitting probability and chance to evade physical attacks.
    • Comparing 100 dex to 200 (same armors/skill%)
    • Comparing 100 magic to 200 (same armors/skill%)
      • Approximately 30% chance to hit (100 attacking 200).
      Approximately 90% chance to hit (200 attacking 100).
